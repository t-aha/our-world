About Us

Our World is bringing the jam band scene to Houston, seamlessly blending psychedelic rock, country, funk, blues, and trance.

Our sound is inspired from bands such as Phish, moe., SCI, Goose, Pink Floyd, the Dead, Spafford, Moses Guest, Widespread Panic, Blues Traveler and more.
